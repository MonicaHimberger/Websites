Frequently asked questions about eSTEAMed:

  • Q: Do you do after school activities for older students?
  • A: At the moment we are developing content for young learners, for elementary and middle students. However, we are considering developing programs for older learners as well.


  • Q: You are not in my child's school, how can I sign up my child for your program?
  • A: At the moment we are only working through school but we are expanding the portfolio of schools we are working with and would love to work with your school's administration to bring eSTEAMed to your child's school.
