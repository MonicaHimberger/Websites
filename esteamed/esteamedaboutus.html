About eSTEAMed

eSTEAMed was born out of love for STEM and creative teaching by two young educators that love to shake things up in the classroom and do things differently. Our goal is to get kids to try Science, Technology, Engineering and Math through the creative lense of Arts. We believe that our kids deserve creative, enriching,well rounded programs that cater to all kinds of learners.

So what is eSTEAMeD?

Our program is tailored for elementary and middle schools. We teach STEM concepts through Arts. Our current curriculum teaches programming concepts through the art of movement and dancing. We use MIT platform called Scratch to animate dance routines created by our students and professional choreographers. In the near future we will tackle other areas of STEM with creative and engaging after school programs that kids actually love

Jacqueline Dugal grew up in West Palm Beach, Florida and received her BFA in Dance Performancend Entrepreneurship from the University of South Florida before moving to New York. A performer, creator, and educator, Jacqueline has taught arts programming to students of all ages from young children to adult. She has danced for multiple companies and projects in NYC while also choreographing work of her own for Dugal Dance Projects.
